Bio

Hi, my name is Bob! My foster mom says I am an amazing dog. I first came into Freedom Dog Rescue as a palliative case but recent test results confirmed that I am cancer free and can find my furever home!

I love to play (I’ve been told I have wonderful energy for my age) but also love to chill out, be lazy and cuddle with my humans. I will follow you everywhere around the house, especially if you are cooking food.

I do get a little scared of some strangers when I first meet them but quickly learn to love and trust them when I see my foster mom does the same. I will need a family who will take me on long walks, lots of play time, and spend enough time at home to hang out with me.

I also need someone who will brush me often as I do shed a lot. I can’t wait to give you all my love and make you as happy as you’ll make me!
